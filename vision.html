About Apple Vision Pro

Apple Vision Pro is Apple's entry into spatial computing — a device that overlays digital content onto the real world using advanced passthrough cameras. Powered by the M2 and R1 chips, it offers an ultra-high-resolution display, eye and hand tracking with no controllers, and deep integration with the Apple ecosystem.
