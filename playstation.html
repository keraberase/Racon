About PlayStation VR2

PlayStation VR2 is Sony's premium VR headset built exclusively for the PS5. It features eye tracking, headset feedback, 3D audio, and the new Sense controllers with adaptive triggers and haptic feedback — bringing a whole new level of immersion to PlayStation gaming.
