What is Virtual Reality?

Virtual Reality (VR) is a technology that creates a simulated environment, placing you inside a fully immersive digital world. Using a VR headset, you can look around, move, and interact as if you were really there. From gaming and education to healthcare and training — VR is changing how we experience the world.

Key Features

Immersive Worlds

Explore realistic 3D environments that feel completely real.

Interactive

Use controllers to touch, grab, and interact with objects.

Medical Use

VR is used for surgery training, therapy, and pain management.

Education

Travel through history or explore the human body in class.
