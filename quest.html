About Meta Quest 3

Meta Quest 3 is the latest standalone VR headset from Meta, featuring mixed reality capabilities that blend the digital and physical worlds. With twice the graphics performance of Quest 2, a slimmer profile, and the new Touch Plus controllers, it delivers the most immersive experience in the Quest lineup.
